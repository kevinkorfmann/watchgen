====================================
Demo: Running SLiM on Simulated Data
====================================

   *Running mini_slim on simulated genomic data.*

.. figure:: /_static/figures/fig_demo_slim.png
   :width: 100%
   :align: center

   **SLiM demo of forward-time simulation.** Panel A: Mean population fitness over generations under neutral and deleterious DFE scenarios. Panel B: Number of segregating sites over time -- purifying selection reduces diversity. Panel C: Mean mutations per individual, showing accumulation under mutation-selection balance. Panel D: Kimura fixation probability as a function of selection coefficient.

This figure was generated by running the ``mini_slim`` implementation
on data simulated with ``msprime``. The script is at
``figures/fig_demo_slim.py``.
